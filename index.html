Reese's Peanut Butter Cups are a chocolate candy made with a peanut butter filling. They were first introduced in 1928 by H.B. Reese, who was a confectioner in Hershey, Pennsylvania. Reese's Peanut Butter Cups are now one of the most popular candies in the world, and they are sold in over 100 countries.

The original Reese's Peanut Butter Cup is made with a milk chocolate shell and a peanut butter filling. However, there are now many different varieties of Reese's Peanut Butter Cups, including white chocolate, dark chocolate, and even peanut butter cups with Reese's Pieces inside.
